Custom Services & Solutions

Monoclonal Antibody Development

Full-service MAb production from antigen preparation to ascites purification across 4 phases with customer evaluation at each stage.

  • Antigen screening & immunization
  • Hybridoma fusion & screening
  • Subcloning & expansion
  • Protein A/G purification

Protein Expression & Purification

Custom recombinant protein expression in E. coli with flexible pricing based on gene complexity and required yield.
